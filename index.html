And Midjourney...

In recent years I've gotten deep into making Midjourney graphics for my articles, building up a library of custom styles along the way, all tuned, repeatable visual recipes refined over hundreds of generations.

I'm happy to make a custom graphic for you, too. Just hit me up and let me know the scene/aesthetic you're looking for, and I'll use one of my styles, or a new one I come up with for you.

Style: Glintsomes

High-contrast illustrations with a dominant electric blue palette, punctuated by vivid accent colors applied with loose, chalky mark-making. Forms are grounded in hard outlines and flat color masses.

Style: Conctos

Flat, graphic illustrations built on a dominant cerulean blue, with warm accent colors (cream, red-orange, gold, etc.) used for precise punctuation and detail.

Style: Metas

Soft, volumetric illustrations built on deep black grounds, with forms emerging from atmospheric gradient lighting and smooth edge blurring. Jewel-tone palettes like emerald, violet, coral, and burnt orange.

Style: Swags

3D renderings defined by their surface materiality. Forms read as lacquered, chrome-plated, or iridescent clay, catching light with a wet, pearlescent sheen. The palette is bold and saturated.

Style: Noons

Bold graphic illustrations anchored by heavy black outlines and flat, saturated color fills. The palette is vivid and warm (cobalt blue, red-orange, forest green) set against single-color grounds that shift across outputs.

Style: Phreaks

Playful mixed-media collage illustrations that stack abstract forms, scribbles, and patterned fragments into dense totemic assemblages, surrounded by generous negative space.

Style: Froods

Flat graphic illustrations with a heavy, uniform grain that breaks saturated color fields into a coarse, dithered texture. The palette is bold and high-key, e.g. cobalt blue, hot pink, forest green, red, yellow.

Style: Coids

Smooth, cleanly rendered anime-ish illustrations with a soft, polished surface quality and no visible grain or texture. The palette is pink-dominant.
